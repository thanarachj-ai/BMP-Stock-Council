BMP team map

One stock. Many lenses. One gate.

BMP Stock Council turns a ticker into a source-backed investment memo by separating research, council judgment, valuation, quality control, reporting, and reusable source curation.

The council desk

Each role owns a specific artifact, so the workflow can disagree with itself productively before the final report speaks in one clear voice.
