Business Model

Business Model

What it means: Target Company is a workflow, cloud, and AI infrastructure company. It makes money when organizations rely on its software to run daily work, store data, build applications, protect systems, and connect employees.

Productivity And Business Processes

Productivity Suite, Collaboration Suite, AI Product, Business Apps, and Professional Network serve knowledge workers, sales teams, finance teams, and recruiters. Customers pay because Target Company is already embedded in how documents, meetings, identity, email, and enterprise workflows operate.

Intelligent Cloud

Growth Platform, server products, Developer Tools, databases, security, and cloud services serve developers, CIOs, AI builders, and enterprises moving workloads to the cloud. Customers pay for scale, reliability, security, developer tools, and integration with the rest of Target Company.

More Personal Computing

Operating System, devices, gaming, search, and advertising serve PC users, OEMs, gamers, advertisers, and consumers. Customers pay because Operating System remains a default enterprise and PC platform, while Consumer Platform and search add consumer and ad exposure.

How it makes money: subscriptions, usage-based cloud consumption, software licenses, advertising, devices, gaming content, and enterprise support. The most important engine is recurring enterprise revenue, especially cloud and Productivity Suite.

Who pays: enterprises, governments, schools, small businesses, developers, PC makers, consumers, advertisers, and game players. The highest-quality customers are enterprises that standardize around Target Company because switching would disrupt identity, files, workflows, security, training, and developer systems.

Why customers choose Target Company instead of others: the products work together, procurement is simpler, security and compliance are enterprise-grade, the partner ecosystem is deep, and employees already know the tools. Target Company is often the safe default choice for large organizations.

Evidence: FY2025 revenue was $281.7B; TTM revenue through FY2026 Q3 was $318.3B; TTM operating margin was 46.8%; TTM operating cash flow was $170.1B. Evidence IDs: E001, E002, E025.

Why it matters: BMP wants businesses where the customer need, payment reason, and competitive position are easy to explain. Target Company passes that first sniff test before the checklist gets stricter.
